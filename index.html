Who We Are

Our Beliefs

Every Vote Counts is a student-led, nonpartisan organization dedicated to increasing voter turnout and expanding voter access nationwide. Our goal for this semester is to achieve 100% voter turnout in the Yale student body.

Voting Is a Universal Right

We are completely nonpartisan, and we believe everybody who can vote, should vote. We regularly work with the Yale Political Union, Yale College Democrats, and Yale College Republicans.
